Pancake Manor

Housed in a charming old building, the restaurant's interior is stunning and the overall setup is delightful, creating a charming dining experience. With a diverse menu offering numerous options, the food is not only delicious but also satisfying.

Food

Pancake Manor in Brisbane offers a wide variety of food options to cater to all tastes. Their signature dish, pancakes, comes in a range of flavors and toppings, while savory options include bacon and eggs, smoked salmon and cream cheese, and ham and cheese.

Drinks

Traditional breakfast lovers can enjoy freshly brewed coffee and fine teas, while those with a sweet tooth can indulge in decadent milkshakes and smoothies. Specialty hot chocolates, iced coffees, and a selection of wines and spirits are also available.

Interior

Housed in a heritage-listed building, the interior design blends vintage and contemporary elements. The high ceilings, exposed brick walls, and stained-glass windows create a grand and elegant atmosphere. The seating arrangement offers a variety of options for different preferences, with well-spaced seating for privacy.
