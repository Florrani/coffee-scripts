Bent Books

Bent Books is a hidden gem for book lovers, offering a wide range of genres and rare finds that you won't come across anywhere else. With its cozy atmosphere and friendly staff, you'll feel right at home as you explore the thousands of books available.

Genres

Bent Books in Brisbane offers a diverse range of genres to cater to all book lovers. From classic literature to contemporary fiction, non-fiction to children's books, and even science fiction, fantasy, and mystery, there is something for everyone at this popular bookstore. Popular genres include classic literature, contemporary fiction, non-fiction, and children's books, with a wide selection of bestsellers and lesser-known authors.

Price

Bent Books in Brisbane offers a diverse range of books at affordable prices, with a focus on second-hand books. They offer a trade-in service and occasional discounts on new books. Pricing may vary based on availability, condition, and demand, but the bookstore's commitment to affordability makes it a popular choice for budget-conscious book lovers.

Interior

Bent Books in Brisbane is a captivating and unique space. The beautifully curated collection of books and nostalgic decor create a whimsical ambiance. The eclectic range of titles and sustainable design reflect the store's commitment to literature and the environment. The warm lighting and quiet atmosphere make it the perfect place to escape into a good book.
